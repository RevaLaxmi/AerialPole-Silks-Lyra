Class Etiquette

  • Arrive Early: Please arrive 5 minutes before class to settle in and prepare.
  • Dress Code: Wear appropriate attire — tights/cycling shorts, sports bra, or fitted shirt. Avoid jewelry, zippers, or anything that may scratch the pole.
  • Hydration: Bring water or electrolytes to stay hydrated, especially during intense sessions.
  • Warm-Up Ready: Ensure you are ready for a quick warm-up before the session begins to avoid injuries.
  • Hygiene: Please wipe down the pole and equipment after use to maintain cleanliness.
  • Respect Others: Be mindful of others in the class, maintain a positive attitude, and encourage fellow students.
